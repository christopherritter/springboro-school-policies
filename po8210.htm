8210 - SCHOOL CALENDAR

The Board of Education recognizes that the preparation of a calendar for the instructional program of the schools is necessary for orderly educational planning and for the efficient operation of the District.

The Board shall determine annually the days when the school shall be in session for instructional purposes.

Such school calendar shall consist of at least the statutory requirements including such time for early dismissal, parent-teacher conferences, professional meetings, and public calamities as is allowed by law and up to three (3) days in order to allow gradual orientation of kindergarten students.

Annually, no later than March, the Superintendent shall prepare a school calendar for Board consideration. Each calendar should be approved fifteen (15) to twenty-four (24) months prior to the actual school year of use.

The Board reserves the right to alter the school calendar.

R.C. 3313.48, 3313.62, 3313.63, 3317.01(B)
