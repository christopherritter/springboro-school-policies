2415 - TUTORING FOR CREDIT

It is the policy of this Board of Education to grant credit for tutoring provided that guidelines of this Board and State requirements are met in full. Tutoring for credit shall be limited to those students whose special needs cannot be met under Policy 2370, Educational Options.

The Superintendent shall be responsible for preparing guidelines to implement this policy.

A.C. 3301-35-01 (D)(5), 3301-35-02 (C)(I)C), 3301-35-02 (C)(3)
