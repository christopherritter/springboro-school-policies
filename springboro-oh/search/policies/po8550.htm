8550 - COMPETITIVE FOOD SALES

The Food and Nutrition Services Department will comply with the provisions set forth in Federal law regarding sale of competitive food and foods of minimal nutritional value.

Only the Food and Nutrition Services Department shall sell food and beverages in District schools during regular school hours.
