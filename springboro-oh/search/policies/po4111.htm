4111 - CREATING A POSITION

The Board of Education recognizes the need to establish positions which, when filled by competent, qualified staff members, will assist the District in achieving the educational goals set by the Board. The District employs only U.S. Citizens and others lawfully authorized to work in the United States.

The Board reserves the right to create new positions and provide each with a job description of the essential functions for which the position was created and provide each with a title that conforms with the appropriate certification insofar as possible.

The Superintendent, shall recommend to the Board the advisability of creating a new position or changing the number of employees in an existing position.
