3130.01 - ASSIGNMENT AND TRANSFER OF ADMINISTRATORS

The Board of Education believes that the judicious placement of administrators within the District is important for the successful functioning of the District.

The Superintendent shall assign administrative staff, with Board approval, as defined in State Statute.

R.C. 3319.01, 3319.12
