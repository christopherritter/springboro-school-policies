4210 - ATTENDANCE

The Board of Education considers attendance an important factor in the successful operation of the School District and in maintaining continuity of the educational program. Regular attendance of each employee shall be considered an important criterion for satisfactory job performance and professional behavior.

As an employee of the District each employee has the responsibility to be on the job on time every scheduled work day. This responsibility requires that the employee maintain good health standards, take intelligent precautions against accidents, both on and off the job, and manage personal affairs in order to satisfy District attendance requirements.
