5605 - SUSPENSION/EXPULSION OF STUDENTS WITH DISABILITIES

In addition to following the requirements and procedures set forth for all students in Policy 5610, the Board of Education shall also abide by State and Federal laws and regulations when disciplining students with disabilities through removal by suspension and/or expulsion.

20 U.S.C. Section 1401 et seq.
Section 504, 1973 Rehab. Act
29 U.S.C. 701 et seq.
34 C.F.R. 300.530-537
