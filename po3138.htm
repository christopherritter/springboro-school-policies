3138 - SUSPENSION DURING PENDING CRIMINAL ACTIONS

The Superintendent shall immediately suspend a licensed professional staff member from all duties that require the care, custody, or control of a child during any pending criminal action for which that staff member has been arrested, summoned and/or indicted for any crimes listed under R.C. 3319.31(C).

If the individual arrested, summoned, and/or indicted for any of the crimes listed under R.C. 3319.31(C) is a person whose duties are assigned by the Treasurer under R.C. 3313.31(B), the Treasurer shall immediately suspend the person from all duties that require the care, custody, or control of a child while the criminal action is pending.
