2432 - DRIVER EDUCATION

The Board of Education may offer a program of driver education. If the Board chooses to offer a program of driver education, in accordance with law the District will charge a fee that is not more than the actual per student cost of providing the program less any subsidy received from the State for driver education. The Board shall set this fee annually if Driver’s Education is offered.
