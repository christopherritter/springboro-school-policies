6470 - PAYMENT OF INVOICES

It is the purpose of the Board of Education to effect the prompt payment of bills, but at the same time ensure that due care has been taken in the review of such bills.

Each bill or obligation of this Board must be fully itemized, verified and duly audited before a warrant can be drawn for its payment.

It shall be the responsibility of the Treasurer upon receipt of an invoice to verify that the voucher is in order, the goods were received in acceptable condition or services were satisfactorily rendered, as determine by the originator of the purchase order, funds are available to cover the payment, the item is one for which the Board budgeted, and the invoice is for the amount contracted.

The Treasurer shall prepare a warrant for payment, cancel the commitment placed against the appropriate account, and post the actual cost.
